Chiral Vibrational Modes in Small Molecules

Authors: Jichen Feng, Ethan Abraham, Joseph Subotnik, Abraham Nitzan
Venue: The Journal of Chemical Physics 163(1): 014106 (2025)

PDF | arXiv | Journal | DOI

Overview

We develop and compare quantitative measures for the chirality of molecular normal modes in small (not necessarily helical) molecules. Building on tools used for helical structures, we apply a continuous chirality measure to mode-induced structures and introduce a pseudoscalar based on atomic linear and angular momentum. We find correlations between mode chirality and structural chirality, and observe distinct handedness behavior across different frequency ranges.
